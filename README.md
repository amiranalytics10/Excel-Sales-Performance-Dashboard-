# Excel-Sales-Performance-Dashboard-

Project Overview:
An interactive Sales & Profit Dashboard built in Excel to analyze retail performance (2014–2017). It transforms raw transaction data into visual, dynamic business insights.

📈 Key Metrics:
Total Sales: $7.24M Total Profit: $893K Orders: 30,361 Avg Ship Days: 3.95 
🔍 Visualizations:
Regional & State Performance: Compares sales across regions (East/West leading) and ranks the top 10 states (led by CA and NY). Product & Logistics Analysis: Tracks Sales vs. Profit by category (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies) and analyzes shipping preferences. Customer Segments: Breakdowns market share via a segment donut chart. 🛠️ 

Excel Skills Used:
Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts Interactive Slicers for dynamic filtering KPI card design & UI alignment
